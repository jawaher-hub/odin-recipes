Lasagna

Description

This lasagna recipe is perfect.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings: fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses: Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
    1. Meat sauce
    2. Noodles
    3. Ricotta mixture
    4. Mozzarella slices
    5. Meat sauce
    6. Parmesan cheese
    7. Repeat the layers, then top with the remaining Parmesan.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.

How Long to Cook Lasagna

  • Cook the assembled lasagna for about 50 minutes in an oven preheated to 375°F (190°C).
  • Cover with foil for the first 25 minutes, then cook uncovered for the last 25 minutes.
  • Let the lasagna rest at room temperature for about 15 minutes before cutting and serving.
